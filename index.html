Frequently Asked Questions

What is DriveQwest?

DriveQwest is an AI-powered driving coach app designed for teens and young drivers. It automatically tracks your trips, provides a DriveScore after each drive, and offers personalized feedback to help you become a safer driver.

How does DriveQwest track my driving?

DriveQwest uses your phone's built-in sensors (GPS, accelerometer, gyroscope) to automatically detect when you're driving. No special hardware needed—just download the app and it works in the background.

What is DriveScore?

DriveScore is a personalized safety score you receive after each trip. It evaluates your driving based on factors like speed, braking, turns, and other safety metrics to help you understand and improve your driving habits.

Will DriveQwest drain my phone battery?

No. DriveQwest is optimized for low battery impact. It uses efficient background processing and only activates when you're driving, ensuring minimal battery drain.

Is DriveQwest safe to use while driving?

Absolutely. DriveQwest runs completely in the background and never requires interaction while you're driving. All feedback and insights are provided before or after your trip to ensure your safety.

Can parents see my driving data?

Yes, DriveQwest offers a parent view feature. Parents can see weekly summaries and trends to support their teen drivers, but you maintain full control over your privacy settings.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. DriveQwest is privacy-focused with all data stored locally on your device. You have full control over your data and can delete or export it at any time. We never share your data without explicit consent.

What devices is DriveQwest available on?

DriveQwest is currently available on iOS devices (iPhone and iPad) through the App Store. The app requires iOS 13.0 or later.

How do I download DriveQwest?

You can download DriveQwest directly from the App Store. Click the "Download on the App Store" button on our website or search for "DriveQwest" in the App Store.
