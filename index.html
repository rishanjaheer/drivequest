Questions, answered

Does DriveQuest require special hardware?

No. Your phone’s sensors are enough. We optimize for low battery impact.

Will the app distract me while I drive?

Never. DriveQuest runs in the background and only interacts before or after your trip.

Who can see my data?

You’re in control. Families can opt in to share improvement metrics with insurers for discounts.
